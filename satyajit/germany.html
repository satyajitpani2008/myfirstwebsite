GERMANY

    • Germany is a country in Central and Western Europe.Germany is the seventh-largest country in Europe.

    • GERMAN is the official language of Germany.

    • Covering an area of 357,022 square kilometres (137,847 sq mi), it lies between the Baltic and North seas to the north, and the Alps to the south.

    • It borders Denmark to the north, Poland and the Czech Republic to the east, Austria and Switzerland to the south, and France, Luxembourg, Belgium, and the Netherlands to the west.

    • Germany has a population of 81 million people.

    • One-third of Germany is still covered in forests and woodlands.

    • Germany is a member of the European Union.

    • university is free for everyone (even non-Germans).

    • There are over 2100 castles in Germany.

    • There are over 1,500 different beers in Germany.
